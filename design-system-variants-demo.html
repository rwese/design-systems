Theme Variants

  • Status: Exploration
  • Updated: 2026-05-14

Compare multiple visual directions for the same documentation concept before choosing one.

Summary Page

Calm

Quiet surfaces, restrained borders, and neutral contrast for operational documents.

  • Best for runbooks
  • Low visual noise

Editorial

Larger type and stronger section rhythm for narrative reports and review documents.

  • Best for updates
  • More expressive hierarchy

Dense

Compact spacing and tighter blocks for facts, decisions, and side-by-side comparison.

  • Best for references
  • Fast scanning
